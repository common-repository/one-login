All networks in one box to login in your blog

== Description ==

Put all social network in one login box for your blog.

Collect all users to your Email Invest account and send free promotions or bulletins to them.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the One-Login plugin to your blog, Activate it, then enter your 'API KEY' from settings menu.
1.1 Get API Key from http://one-login.info
2. Use WIDGET to place your login box.

== Frequently asked questions ==

= How to add network button =

1. Open One-Login.info
2. Sign Up.
3. Add your blog from menu 'New Website'
4. Open Settings for your site and activate/Configure your networks.

= How to use Email Invest =
1. Open http://one-login.info and sign in.
2. From your account settings, enter your API and List_ID
3. It's done
